Professional Midjourney Guide

1

Getting Started with Discord

Midjourney operates through Discord. Here's how to get set up:

  1. Create a Discord account at discord.com
  2. Join the Midjourney Discord server at discord.gg/midjourney
  3. Subscribe to a Midjourney plan at midjourney.com/account
  4. Use /imagine commands in any #newbies channel
2

Writing Effective Prompts

Craft prompts that generate professional, usable images:

/imagine professional headshot, business executive, clean white background, studio lighting, sharp focus, corporate photography style --ar 1:1 --v 6

Key Components:

  • Subject description
  • Style and mood
  • Lighting and composition
  • Technical parameters

Useful Parameters:

  • --ar (aspect ratio)
  • --v (version)
  • --style (stylization level)
  • --quality (rendering quality)
